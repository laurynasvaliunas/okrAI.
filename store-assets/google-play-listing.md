## Full Description (4000 chars max)

okrAI brings the OKR goal-setting framework — used by Google, Intel, and the world's best teams — to your personal life.

Set objectives that actually mean something. Break them into measurable key results. Get AI coaching every step of the way.

★ GOALS THAT WORK
Most goal apps let you write lists. okrAI makes you define what success looks like — so you always know exactly how far you've come. Whether it's running a marathon, saving money, or learning a new skill, every goal has a clear finish line.

★ YOUR PERSONAL AI COACH
Stuck on where to start? Not sure if your goal is realistic? The built-in AI coach gives personalised, context-aware advice based on your actual goals — not generic tips. Chat any time, ask anything.

★ ORGANISED BY LIFE AREA
Set goals across Health, Career, Finance, Relationships, Learning, Creativity, Mindfulness, and Lifestyle. Customise which life areas appear first so your priorities are always visible.

★ INSTANT PROGRESS TRACKING
A live progress ring on every goal. A quick-update sheet for key results. A home screen that shows your overall goal health at a glance. No digging, no spreadsheets.

★ CALM, PREMIUM DESIGN
Built for focus: no ads, no distracting badges, no dark patterns. A beautiful dark interface with smooth animations, haptic feedback, and full Material You dark mode support.

FEATURES:
✓ Unlimited personal objectives with key results
✓ Weekly, monthly, or quarterly goal cadence

✓ AI-powered hints as you write your goals
