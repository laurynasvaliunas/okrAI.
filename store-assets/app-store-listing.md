## App Store Description (4000 chars max)

okrAI turns the OKR goal-setting framework used by Google, Intel, and the world's top teams into a simple, beautiful personal habit.

Set objectives that matter. Break them into measurable key results. Let AI coach you every step of the way.

**GOALS THAT ACTUALLY STICK**
Most goal apps let you write a wish list. okrAI makes you define what success looks like — so you always know if you're winning. Whether it's running a marathon, saving €5 000, or reading 24 books this year, every goal gets a clear finish line.

**YOUR AI COACH, ALWAYS ON**
Stuck on how to break a goal into action steps? Not sure if your target is realistic? The built-in AI coach gives personalised, context-aware guidance — not generic motivation quotes. Ask anything. Get a real answer.

**LIFE AREAS THAT FIT YOU**
Organise goals across the areas that matter most: Health, Career, Finance, Relationships, Learning, Creativity, Mindfulness, and Lifestyle. Prioritise your own life areas so the most important ones stay front and centre.

**TRACK PROGRESS AT A GLANCE**
Every goal shows a live progress ring. Every key result has a simple slider update. Your home screen gives you an instant health check across all your life areas — no digging, no spreadsheets.

**BEAUTIFUL & FOCUSED**
Designed with care: no ads, no clutter, no gamification tricks. Just you and your goals, in a calm, premium interface that gets out of your way.

**WHAT YOU CAN DO**
• Create unlimited personal objectives with key results
• Choose weekly, monthly, or quarterly cadence
• Update progress in seconds with the built-in update sheet
• Get AI-powered hints as you write your goals
• Chat with your personal AI coach at any time
• Filter goals by life area
• Pull-to-refresh, haptic feedback, full dark mode

**PREMIUM**
Unlock unlimited goals and full AI coach access with okrAI Premium.
Monthly and annual plans available. Manage or cancel anytime in your App Store settings.
